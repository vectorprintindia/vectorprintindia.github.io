Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services

What file formats do you deliver?

We deliver files in all standard vector formats including AI, EPS, PDF, and SVG. For specific needs, we can also provide files in DXF, DWG, or other specialized formats.

How long does a typical project take?

Simple vector tracing projects are typically completed within 24-48 hours. More complex projects like logo design or website development may take 1-3 weeks depending on the scope and revisions.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes, we offer unlimited revisions until you're completely satisfied with the final result. Our goal is to ensure you love the designs we create for you.

What if I need rush service?

We offer expedited services for urgent projects. Rush service typically adds a 50% premium to the standard pricing and guarantees completion within 12-24 hours for vector tracing projects.

How do I get started?

Simply contact us through our website, email, or WhatsApp with your project details. We'll provide a free quote and timeline for your project.
